# Our Security Commitment
IBRA takes the security of our platform and users' data very seriously.
We are committed to responsible disclosure and working with security researchers.

## Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability on IBRA,
please report it responsibly by following our security policy.

### How to Report

Email: security@ibra.biz.id
PGP Key: https://ibra.biz.id/security-key.asc
Response Time: Within 48 hours

Please include the following information in your report:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Your contact information (optional)

## Scope

In-scope:
- ibra.biz.id (main website)
- api.ibra.biz.id (API endpoints)
- Mobile applications

Out-of-scope:
- Third-party services
- Social media accounts
- Physical security

## Responsible Disclosure Guidelines

We ask that you:
1. Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have had 60 days to respond
2. Do not access, modify, or delete user data
3. Do not perform testing that could affect other users
4. Do not use social engineering or phishing attacks
5. Do not install any persistent backdoors
6. Report vulnerabilities through proper channels

## Our Commitment

We commit to:
1. Respond to all reports within 48 hours
2. Keep you informed of our progress
3. Not take legal action against responsible researchers
4. Give appropriate credit in our security hall of fame (with permission)
5. Work with you to verify and fix the vulnerability

## Bug Bounty Program

Currently, IBRA does not have a monetary bug bounty program.
However, we recognize responsible disclosure with:
- Public acknowledgment (with permission)
- Listed in our security researchers hall of fame
- Exclusive IBRA merchandise for significant findings

## Safe Harbor

We consider activities conducted in accordance with this policy
to be authorized and will not pursue legal action against researchers.
